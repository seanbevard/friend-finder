"You don't have to be lonely, with LionsOnly."

Orlando City SC is proud to announce the launch of Lions Only, a dating website built exclusively for City fans. The state-of-the-art matchmaking system uses a bank of 3.5 billion algorithms to determine your perfect match. The technology, designed by industry visionary Lorenzo von Matterhorn, is used to power similar websites around the world.

“We want our fan base to span generations,” said Orlando City Founder and President Phil Rawlins. “Lions Only is an obvious step in that direction and will stand to unite our fans in a way no other club has done before.”
